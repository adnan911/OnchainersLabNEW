## 1. Overview

Color Maze is a blockchain-enabled game that interacts with the Solana blockchain. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and important information about blockchain transactions, fees, risks, and refunds.

By using Color Maze, you agree to this Privacy Policy.

---

## 2. Information We Collect

### 2.1 Wallet Address and Blockchain Activity

When you connect a Solana wallet (such as Phantom or Solflare), we may process:

- Your public wallet address
- Transaction signatures that you approve
- Public blockchain transaction data related to in-game assets (such as Maze Matter NFT)

We **never collect or store**:

- Private keys
- Seed phrases
- Wallet recovery phrases

Your wallet provider handles authentication and transaction signing.

---

### 2.2 Local Storage

Color Maze uses local storage on your device to:

- Save game settings
- Store level progress
- Save selected skins or preferences
- Improve performance and user experience

This data is stored locally on your device and is not stored on our servers.

---

### 2.3 Technical Information

We may collect limited technical information such as:

- Device type (Web or Android)
- Operating system
- Browser type
- Error logs and crash reports

This information is used only to improve stability and performance. We do not collect personal identity information.

---

## 3. How We Use Information

We use collected information to:

- Verify ownership of in-game assets (NFTs/skins)
- Process Maze Matter NFT minting
- Save game progress
- Prevent fraud, abuse, or exploitation
- Improve game performance and stability

## 4. Blockchain and Public Data

Color Maze interacts with public blockchain networks.

You understand and agree that:

- Blockchain transactions are public
- Blockchain transactions are permanent
- Blockchain transactions cannot be reversed
- We cannot delete or modify blockchain data
- Wallet addresses and transaction history may be publicly visible

---

## 5. Fees & Transparency

When you mint or purchase in-game blockchain assets, you may be required to pay fees.

These may include:

- Solana network fees
- NFT protocol fees
- Color Maze service or maintenance fee (if applicable)

All fees are displayed before you approve the transaction in your wallet.

By approving a transaction, you authorize the blockchain to transfer the required fees.

**All blockchain fees are paid directly to the network and are not controlled by OnchainersLab.**

---

## 6. Refund Policy

Due to the nature of blockchain transactions:

- All blockchain transactions are **final and irreversible**
- We **cannot cancel, reverse, or refund** any transaction once it is confirmed on the blockchain
- This includes:
  - NFT minting fees
  - Network fees
  - Protocol fees
  - In-game blockchain purchases

If a transaction fails, the blockchain network may still charge a small network fee. These fees are not controlled by us and cannot be refunded.

Please make sure you review all transactions carefully before approving them in your wallet.

By minting or purchasing NFTs in Color Maze, you acknowledge and accept the following risks:

- Blockchain transactions are irreversible
- NFTs may have no resale value
- NFT prices may be volatile
- Network congestion may cause failed or delayed transactions
- Wallet software may contain bugs
- Third-party services may experience downtime
- Losing your wallet private key means losing access to your assets permanently

Color Maze and OnchainersLab do **not** guarantee:

- NFT value
- NFT resale market
- Profit or financial return
- Continuous availability of blockchain services

NFTs are digital collectibles and may carry financial risk.
